Korean Exchange Listing Criteria (2025)

According to HighCrypto's official research report, major Korean exchanges have confirmed specific listing requirements. Both Upbit and Coinone have officially announced community activity as a critical evaluation criterion.

Upbit Official Criteria

  • Community Activity Evaluation: "Whether token distribution is not excessively concentrated among a few, information disclosure and community activity, etc."
  • Project Transparency: Legal entity existence and active development community

Coinone Official Criteria

  • 'Domestic Community' explicitly listed as 1 of 7 key listing criteria (July 2021 announcement)
  • 7 Total Criteria: Business sustainability, governance transparency, token distribution, global marketability, domestic community, team composition, project progress
